## Overview

**Voyyara** is a production-grade AI travel planning platform that demonstrates the full power of the **Contentstack Digital Experience Platform**. Users can chat naturally with "Voyyara Genie" to receive personalized, day-by-day travel itineraries tailored to their preferences and budget.

**[Try the Live Demo](https://voyyara-ai.eu-contentstackapps.com/)**

---

## Contentstack Products Integrated

This project showcases **8 Contentstack products** working together to deliver a complete digital experience:

| Product | Usage |
|---------|-------|
| **Headless CMS** | Central content repository for destinations, itineraries, attractions, and editorial content |
| **TypeScript Delivery SDK** | Type-safe content queries with full IntelliSense support |
| **Generative AI API** | Powers the AI travel assistant with intelligent itinerary generation |
| **Brand Kit** | Voice profile ensuring all AI content matches Voyyara's friendly, expert tone |
| **Personalize** | Delivers audience-based content variants (luxury vs. budget experiences) |
| **Live Preview** | Real-time content editing experience for marketers |
| **Launch** | Production hosting with automatic Git-based deployments |
| **Automate** | Workflow automation for email notifications on enquiries |

---

## Key Features

- **AI Travel Assistant** - Chat naturally with Voyyara Genie to plan your dream trip
- **Smart Itineraries** - Day-by-day plans with timing, activities, and recommendations
- **Personalized Experiences** - Content adapts to user preferences and travel style
- **Taxonomy-Based Filtering** - Browse destinations by type, region, and season
- **Multi-Destination Support** - Vietnam, Japan, Thailand, Singapore, South Korea, and more
- **Real-Time Preview** - Editors see content changes instantly before publishing
- **Responsive Design** - Beautiful experience on any device
- **Automated Workflows** - Email notifications and content workflows
